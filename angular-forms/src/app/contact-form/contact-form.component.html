Dates will be allocated according to age.

Everyone over the age of 5 (inclusive) can apply for vaccination. In the case of children aged 5 to 17, their legal representatives can apply for vaccination.

Vaccines are given primarily by age.

At the request of the legal representative, persons from 5 to 17 years of age (inclusive) are allocated vaccines - Comirnaty (Pfizer / BioNTech).
Vaccines from 18 years of age (inclusive) and older are given vaccines - Spikevax (Moderna), Comirnaty (Pfizer / BioNTech), Nuvaxovid (Novavax), Janssen (Johnson & Johnson).
